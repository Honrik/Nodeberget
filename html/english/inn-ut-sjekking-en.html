Check in/out

What To Do

Check In

Normal check-in at the holiday home is at 14:00 on the day of arrival, according to procedures in AirBnB . If you would like a different check-in time, this can be arranged. After the agreement is finished, you will be sent an electronic key code so that you can check in yourself.

Check out

Normal check-out is at 12:00 on the last day. At a different check-out time, this must be arranged. At check-out, the following must be done:

  • The holiday home must be cleared
  • The dishwasher must be emptied, and cutlery/service must be put in place
  • The tables and kitchen counter must be cleared/dried off
  • All rubbish and empty goods must be emptied/removed – and rubbish must be wrapped in bags and disposed of in waste containers. Rubbish containers can be found in the parking lot close by.
  • If you have used towels – these must be washed and cleaned back
  • The grill is cleaned (if it has been used)
  • Kayaks and Paddle Boards are laid in place
