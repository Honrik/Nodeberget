Tourist Attractions

Experiences

On Flekkerøya, there are beautiful outdoor areas with small, idyllic coves, beaches, and hiking trails for public use. We recommend visiting "Dyreparken" or Badeland, Climbing Centre in Kristiansand, "Kanonmuseum" at Møvik, or the unique Michelin-starred restaurant under the water, "Under," located at Lindesnes.

Or how about a trip to the charming "Fiskebrygga in Kristiansand," with its delicacies from the sea.

Festivals

Kristiansand has many festivals and concerts throughout the year, especially in the summer. Here you will find Summer Breeze, Seagull Scream, Palmesus, Ravnedalen Live, or you can visit the cultural center in Kristiansand, with all its activities.

Sports & Training

There is a separate training arena at Flekkerøy, only 2 kilometers from the holiday home. Furthermore, there are several gyms in the area, including "Crossfit Vågsbygd", and the fitness center Sats "Aquarama Kristiansand".
