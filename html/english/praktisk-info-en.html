Practical info

Here you will find practical information for those who are going to rent the vacation house

MAIN DOOR

The main door has an electronic key lock with a code. The code must be used by opening/ locking the door. Note: When pulling through, the door must be held / locked so that it does not slam shut.

SLIDING

The sliding doors are opened / closed by pushing the handle to the right / against the wall with both hands. I.e. one hand on the door and one hand on the handle. Then turn the knob to the left. Then the door opens. NOTE! Make sure the handle is pointing down when using the door!

GAS FIREPLACES

The gas fireplaces are started/closed by their own remote control. The gas for the gas fireplaces is placed outside between the outdoor shed and the cabin towards the mountain / parking lot in a closet. There is also a gas fireplace that can be used for barbecues outside.

INTERNET

There is internet in the cabin. Network name and password will be assigned at check-in.

MUSIC / SONOS

There are integrated Sonos speakers on the 1st floor of the holiday home that can be accessed via the Internet.

TV

It's a large smart TV that can be accessed via Chromecast.

GARBAGE

All rubbish must be wrapped in bags – and disposed of in trash cans in the parking lot. The key to these bins is in the holiday home.

SUMMER PERIOD

During the summer period, rubbish is thrown into containers at the parking lot. It is recycling: Paper/cardboard in 'green' containers. Other waste in 'grey' containers. Wine bottles, glasses are thrown into separate containers

NOVEMBER TO APRIL

During the winter period, all rubbish must be disposed of in containers placed at the cabin renovation point at the turning point at the end of Skibbuveien, Skålevig. The same source sorting applies to: Paper/cardboard in "green" containers. Other waste in "grey" containers. Wine bottles and glasses must be disposed of in separate containers located at the cabin waste collection point at the turning point at the end of Skibbuveien, Skålevig. This is located on the road midway between the cottage and the roundabout (Kiwi shop). Skibbuveien, located on the old main pier at Flekkerøy, down to the sea (about 200 meters from the main road).
