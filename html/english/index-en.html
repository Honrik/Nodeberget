Welcome to Nodeberget!

The charming holiday home is surrounded by beautiful fine rocks for sunbathing and with a fantastic panoramic view of the entrance to Kristiansand. Here you get tranquility and nature experiences in luxurious surroundings by the sea. The holiday home is located a short drive from Kristiansand and has access to a charming bathing place with polished rocks. The home is sunny and is the perfect place to enjoy lovely summer days both inside and outside. Inside, the cozy fireplace, large windows and modern comforts create a welcoming atmosphere. The perfect place for relaxation and unforgettable moments in maritime surroundings - summer as winter.


The modern holiday home is approximately 240 m2, with state-of-the-art kitchen, 2 living rooms, 3 bathrooms and 4 bedrooms. You will also have access to 2 parking spaces and boat space. Here you will find more information about the holiday home, including different packages/ prices, directions, as well as practical information for you as a renter.
