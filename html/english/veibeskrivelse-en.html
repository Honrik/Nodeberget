Veien til Nodeberget

Årekjerret 93 is about 20 minutes' drive from Kristiansand city center.

  • Drive on E39 from Kristiansand towards Vågsbygd (towards Stavanger from Kristiansand). Follow Highway 456 towards Vågsbygd for about 6.7 km.

  • Drive through the tunnel to Flekkerøya - approximately 2.3 km long.

  • At the roundabout on Flekkerøya, take the 3rd exit to Skålevik. Follow this road as far as it goes - when it narrows down at the very end, turn left to Årekjerret.

  • Follow Årekjerret as far as it goes and turn right up a small hill. You'll arrive at a private parking lot. Park your car near the garage, close to the stairs leading up to Årekjerret 93. You can also park in front of the garage. Årekjerret 93 is the cabin located "directly above" the garage on the left side.
